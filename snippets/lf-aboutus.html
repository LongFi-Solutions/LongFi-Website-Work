Why interconnectivity fails

Outdoor cellular networks are not designed to perform well inside buildings. Dense materials, layout complexity, and user density degrade signal quality, even when devices show full bars.

The LongFi Solutions approach

LongFi enables carriers to route mobile traffic over venue Wi‑Fi indoors. By using Wi‑Fi as a transport layer, we help deliver more reliable calls, messages, and data inside buildings.

Our operating principles

Built on integrity

LongFi designs each deployment with security and reliability as core requirements. We align with established network engineering standards to ensure stable, isolated, and carrier compatible connectivity that performs consistently in real world environments.

Enabled by innovation

Driven by teamwork
