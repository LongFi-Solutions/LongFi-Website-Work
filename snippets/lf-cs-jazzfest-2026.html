The Challenge

When a festival gets packed, phones stop working.

Shoulder to shoulder with the crowd, a phone just will not load. At JazzFest the tickets live in an app, so a phone that cannot connect means a guest who cannot get through the gate.

Tickets will not load at the gate

Guests stand in line waiting for a mobile ticket to load, right at the start of the day.

Payments stall at the lines

Food and drink payments time out when the grounds are packed.

Groups lose each other

Messages and location shares fail, so friends cannot regroup.

The Solution

LongFi Connect improves cell connectivity across the crowd using extra Wi-Fi capacity on site.

Coverage focused on the busiest spots, so phones connect on their own and stay working even when the grounds are packed.

  • Automatic for guests. Phones connect on their own, with no password, no app, and no sign-up. It feels just like cellular.
  • Right where it counts. Coverage focused on the gates, the food and drink lines, and the stage crowds.
  • Built for big crowds. Tuned to hold clear, voice-grade quality even when the grounds are packed.
  • Secure by design. Guest traffic is authenticated and encrypted, and kept separate from the venue's systems.
  • Ready fast. Weatherproof gear that sets up quickly, built for the outdoors.
