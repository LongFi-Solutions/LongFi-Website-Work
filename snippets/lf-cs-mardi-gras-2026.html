The Challenge

When the crowd peaks, guests lose signal inside the venue.

When the Quarter fills up, phones struggle inside a packed venue, right when guests are paying, posting, and calling rides.

Payments can stall

Tap-to-pay and mobile orders can time out when the network is busiest.

Moments go unshared

Guests may miss the chance to post the night out that markets the venue.

Rides get harder to call

Weak signal makes rideshare and callbacks unreliable at the door.

The Solution

LongFi Connect improves indoor cell coverage using each venue's spare Wi-Fi capacity.

It went on as a configuration on each venue's existing Wi-Fi, set up site by site and switched on ahead of Mardi Gras.

  • Runs on the Wi-Fi in place. A configuration on the network the venue already pays for.
  • Connects on the SIM. Phones attach on their own, with no app, no password, and no portal login.
  • Carrier-approved, set by area. Approved by the carrier and tuned venue by venue before the crowds arrived.
  • Secure by design. Guest traffic is authenticated and encrypted, and kept apart from venue systems.
  • No added cost. The Wi-Fi already in place carries the traffic and can return monthly value.
