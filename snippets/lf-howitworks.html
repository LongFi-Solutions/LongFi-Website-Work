Keep your network secure.

LongFi Connect follows enterprise grade security standards recognized by the Wi‑Fi Alliance®.

Automatic Authentication

Passpoint allows devices to connect securely and automatically via EAP, eliminating passwords and insecure open guest networks.

SIM-based Access

EAP verifies each device before it joins the network, providing controlled 802.1X access with enterprise security.

Encrypted Connections

WPA3 Enterprise encryption protects every wireless connection with the same standards used in banks and airports.

Secure Transport

RadSec (TLS) encrypts all authentication and accounting messages over the internet for secure communication with carriers.
