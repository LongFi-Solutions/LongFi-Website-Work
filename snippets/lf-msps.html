Enterprise‑grade by design.

LongFi Connect is built on industry-standard authentication and network protocols. As a member of the Wireless Broadband Alliance, all implementations follow WBA-certified standards and best practices. Deployments operate within defined network boundaries and align with existing infrastructure configurations, without introducing new exposure to internal systems.

Authentication & Connectivity
