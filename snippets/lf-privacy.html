Privacy Policy

Effective June 24, 2026

This policy explains what information we collect when you submit a form on our website, why we collect it, and how we use it. We keep this short and clear on purpose. If anything is unclear, contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

Who we are

LongFi Solutions Inc. helps venues and partners use the Wi‑Fi they already have to deliver better indoor cell connectivity. When this policy says "we," "us," or "LongFi," it refers to LongFi Solutions Inc.

What we collect

When you fill out a form on our site, we collect the details you choose to give us, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your business email
  • Your company or venue name
  • A phone number, if you provide one

We also record which page of our website you submitted the form from, so we can understand your interest and respond in context.

Why we collect it

We use your information for a limited set of purposes:

  • To evaluate your property or venue for compatibility with LongFi Connect
  • To evaluate the connectivity improvement for your site
  • To contact you about your inquiry and next steps

What we do not do

We do not sell or rent your information. We do not share it for advertising, and we do not use it for anything beyond evaluating your site and communicating with you about LongFi. We treat the information you give us with care and only use it for the reasons above.

Who can access it

Your information is accessible to the LongFi team and to trusted service providers who help us operate our business, such as our customer relationship and communication tools. These providers are expected to protect your information and use it only to provide their services to us.

How long we keep it

We keep your information for as long as needed to evaluate your site, support our conversation with you, and meet our legal and operational requirements. You can ask us to remove your information at any time.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information you submitted. Just contact us using the details below and we will take care of it.

Consent

By submitting a form on our website, you agree to this privacy policy and to our use of your information as described here.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.
